IDeepify

IDeepify is a project that targets fintech industry. Using IDeepify, you can scan the face and the back of the Egyptian national identity card, take a selfie, and get your data extracted and your picutre matched with the one on the ID.

IDeepify is deep learning-based. Finding training data for such a specific task was quite challenging. around 300+ images were found online. Some data was gathered from friends.But the major portion of data was synthetic.

    IDeepify consists of four parts:
  • ID localization and cropping: Detects the face or the back of the ID, extracts the corners, and crops the ID.
  • Face verification: Extracts the face picutre from the ID and verifies it is the same person in the selfie picutre.
  • Text segmentation: A neural network-based filter removes the background and only keeps the text to ease the OCR step.
  • OCR: Optical character recognition step, and the final one.
Second stage of face verification utilizes a collection of pre-trained face verification models. The "meta-model" was then finetuned with a few pairs of (Selfie, ID). Finally, the total accuracy was tested with FaceScrub Dataset and it was 92%.
