Automated Production Line

This was a project for "Design of mechatronic systems 1" course at Ain Shams university. The project was to build an assembly/disasembly production line. The targets were to:

  • Make it as simple and cheap as possible.
  • Classify the products based on a physical feature. Our feature was the color of the lower part.
  • Keep the production line in an endless loop.
Our project won the first place as it had the simplest design, lowest cost, and the smallest footprint of all projects in the course.
